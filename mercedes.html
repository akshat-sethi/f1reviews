Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One team has been the most dominant team in the last decade, with 6 constructor's titles in a row. In the 2019 season they attained 739 points, 235 points more than Ferrari who placed second. Their current drivers are Lewis Hamilton and Valtteri Bottas, with team principal Toto Wolff.
