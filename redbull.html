RedBull Racing

Home | Mercedes-AMG Petronas | Scuderia Ferrari | RedBull Racing

Aston Martin RedBull Racing is the second most successful team in the last decade. They have attained 4 constructor's titles in a row from 2010 to 2013. They also won 4 driver's championship titles with Sebastian Vettel. In the 2019 season they attained 417 points. Their current drivers are Max Verstappen and Alexander Albon (who joined RedBull Racing in Belgium from sister team Toro Rosso), with team principal Christian Horner.
