Scuderia Ferrari

Home | Mercedes-AMG Petronas | Scuderia Ferrari | RedBull Racing

Scuderia Ferrari is the most successful team in the history of Formula 1. They match Mercedes's 6 constructor's titles in a row and have 14 constructor's titles in total, the most of any team. They had Michael Schumacher who won 5 titles with them. In the 2019 season they attained 504 points. Their current drivers are Sebastian Vettel and Charles Leclerc, with team principal Mattia Binotto.
